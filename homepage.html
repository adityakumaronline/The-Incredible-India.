Indian Diversity

India is a country of great racial diversity, with a wide variety of different ethnic and linguistic groups. The population is primarily an admixture of the following races: Indo-Aryans, Dravidians, and Mongoloids.

Indian Culture

Indian culture is a combination of social norms and technologies that originated in India. It's one of the world's oldest cultures, dating back about 4,500 years. Indian culture is a combination of various subcultures and century-old traditions.

Indian Heritage
